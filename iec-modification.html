IEC Modification Guide (DGFT Portal)

IEC holders must update their Import Export Code details whenever there is a change in business information. DGFT allows modification of IEC profile through the online portal.

Common reasons for IEC modification include:

  • Change of business address
  • Change of bank account details
  • Change of email or mobile number
  • Addition or removal of partners/directors
  • Change in nature of business

IEC Modification Fee

The fee for modifying IEC details is:

₹200

Payment is made online through the DGFT portal.

Steps to Modify IEC Details

Step 1 — Visit DGFT Portal

Open DGFT website:

https://dgft.gov.in

Step 2 — Login to Your Account

Enter your registered email and password to access the DGFT dashboard.

Step 3 — Navigate to IEC Profile Management

Go to:

Services → IEC Profile Management → Manage IEC

Step 4 — Validate IEC

Enter your IEC number and validate using:

  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
  • Aadhaar e-sign

Step 5 — Modify Required Fields

You can update the following details:

  • Firm address
  • Bank account details
  • Email or mobile number
  • Branch details
  • Partner or director details

Step 6 — Upload Supporting Documents

Upload documents if required such as:

  • Address proof
  • Cancelled cheque
  • Partnership deed update

Documents must be uploaded in PDF format (max size 5MB).

Step 7 — Sign and Submit Application

Submit the application using:

  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
  • Aadhaar e-Sign

Cases Requiring DGFT Regional Authority Approval

Some IEC modifications require approval from DGFT Regional Authority (RA).

Examples include:

  • Change in PAN of firm
  • Change in nature of concern
  • Addition or removal of partners/directors

In such cases, application status may show:

In Progress

until RA approves the request.

Important Notes

  • IEC modification may take 12–24 hours to reflect in DGFT systems.
  • Some updates require verification by DGFT Regional Authority.
  • Changes should be made immediately when business information changes.

IEC Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

This section answers common questions related to Import Export Code (IEC), DGFT portal usage, IEC modification and compliance requirements.

General IEC Questions

What is Import Export Code (IEC)?

IEC is a 10-digit identification number issued by DGFT which is required for businesses involved in import or export activities.

Is IEC mandatory for exporters?

Yes, IEC is mandatory for most import and export activities except in limited cases such as government departments.

How much time does it take to get IEC?

IEC is usually issued immediately after successful submission of the application on the DGFT portal.

What is the IEC application fee?

The fee for IEC application is ₹500.

DGFT Portal Questions

Why is registration required on DGFT portal?

Registration allows users to manage IEC profile, submit applications, track status and receive notifications through the DGFT dashboard.

What is the username for DGFT login?

Your registered email ID becomes the username for DGFT portal login.

How long does OTP remain valid?

The OTP sent during DGFT registration remains valid for 15 minutes.

Which browsers support the DGFT portal?

The portal works best with latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge and Apple Safari.

IEC Linking Questions

How can I link my existing IEC to DGFT portal?

Login to DGFT portal and navigate to Services → Link IEC. Enter IEC number and authenticate using DSC or Aadhaar e-sign.

Can I link multiple IECs with one login?

No. A user can link only one IEC with a single DGFT login profile.

IEC Modification Questions

How can I modify IEC details?

Login to DGFT portal and navigate to Services → IEC Profile Management → Modify IEC. Update required details and submit application.

What is the fee for IEC modification?

The fee for IEC modification is ₹200.

Why is IEC modification showing “In Progress”?

Some modifications require approval from DGFT Regional Authority such as change of PAN, change in nature of firm or change in partner details.

Bank Validation Questions

What is bank account validation in IEC?

Bank account details are validated through NPCI system to ensure the account belongs to the IEC holder.

Why is bank validation showing “In Progress”?

Bank validation depends on the bank response and may take a few minutes to several days depending on the bank.

IEC Certificate Questions

How can I print IEC certificate?

Login to DGFT portal and go to Services → IEC → Manage IEC → Print IEC.

When will IEC reflect on ICEGATE?

IEC usually reflects on ICEGATE in real time once it is issued.

IEC Suspension Questions

Why can IEC be suspended?

IEC may be suspended due to failure to update IEC annually, incorrect details or regulatory compliance issues.

How can I revoke IEC suspension?

Login to DGFT portal and apply for revocation through IEC Profile Management section.

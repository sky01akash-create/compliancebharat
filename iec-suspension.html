IEC Suspension & Revocation Guide

Importer Exporter Code (IEC) may be suspended or deactivated by DGFT due to compliance issues or failure to update IEC information. When an IEC is suspended, exporters and importers cannot carry out international trade transactions until the issue is resolved.

Common Reasons for IEC Suspension

IEC may be suspended for several reasons, including:

  • Failure to update IEC annually on DGFT portal
  • Incorrect or incomplete IEC profile details
  • Non-compliance with DGFT regulations
  • Regulatory investigation or enforcement action
  • Inactive or invalid bank account validation

Impact of IEC Suspension

If IEC is suspended, the following activities may be affected:

  • Export shipments may not be processed
  • Import clearances may be blocked at customs
  • Export incentives and schemes may not be available
  • International payments related to trade may face compliance restrictions

How to Check IEC Status

You can check your IEC status through DGFT portal.

Visit:

https://dgft.gov.in

Login and navigate to:

Services → IEC Profile Management → View IEC Details

Steps to Apply for Revocation of IEC Suspension

Step 1 — Login to DGFT Portal

Visit the DGFT website and login using your registered email ID and password.

Step 2 — Access IEC Profile Management

Navigate to:

Services → IEC Profile Management

Step 3 — Link IEC (if not already linked)

If IEC is not linked with your login account, link it first before applying for revocation.

Step 4 — Submit Revocation Request

Select the option to apply for revocation of suspended IEC and provide required details.

Step 5 — Upload Supporting Documents

Upload any documents required to resolve the compliance issue.

Documents must be uploaded in PDF format (max file size 5MB).

Step 6 — Sign Application

Submit the revocation request using:

  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
  • Aadhaar e-Sign

Step 7 — DGFT Regional Authority Review

The request will be reviewed by the concerned DGFT Regional Authority (RA).

Once approved, the IEC suspension will be revoked.

Important Notes

  • Ensure IEC profile details are accurate before submitting revocation request.
  • Annual IEC update is mandatory to avoid future suspension.
  • Revocation approval is subject to DGFT verification.
